ABOUT FLINSTONE

A Dubai trading house for precious metals and stones.

Flinstone Trading FZCO operates from the Dubai Multi Commodities Centre (DMCC), one of the world's principal hubs for precious trade. The desk deals in gold, silver, platinum, and palladium, alongside certified diamonds, coloured stones, and natural pearls, sourced from accredited refineries and mines, and placed with bullion houses and institutional buyers across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

Positioned at the Center of Global Commodities Trade

Operating from DMCC enables Flinstone Trading FZCO to participate in one of the world’s most established precious commodities ecosystems. The company connects trusted international suppliers with regional and global buyers while maintaining disciplined sourcing standards, regulatory alignment, and transparent trade practices across its operations.
