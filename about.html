ABOUT FLINSTONE

Precious Commodities Trading from Dubai’s DMCC Hub

Flinstone Trading FZCO is a Dubai-based commodities trading company operating from the Dubai Multi Commodities Centre (DMCC). The company focuses on the sourcing and international supply of precious metals, certified diamonds, natural pearls, and colored gemstones through verified global partners, supporting refineries, bullion traders, and institutional buyers across key markets.

Positioned at the Center of Global Commodities Trade

Operating from DMCC enables Flinstone Trading FZCO to participate in one of the world’s most established precious commodities ecosystems. The company connects trusted international suppliers with regional and global buyers while maintaining disciplined sourcing standards, regulatory alignment, and transparent trade practices across its operations.
