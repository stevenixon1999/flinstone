Pearls

Natural and cultured pearls across Akoya, South Sea, Tahitian, and Freshwater varieties, graded for lustre, surface, and size, and sourced from established pearl farms.

Certified diamonds graded across the Four Cs and backed by GIA, IGI, or HRD reports, sourced from mines and cutting centres in both rough and polished form.

Emeralds

Emeralds from Colombian, Zambian, and Brazilian origins, certified and graded for colour and clarity with treatment status disclosed, sourced at mine level.
