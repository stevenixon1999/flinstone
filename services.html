Flinstone Trading FZCO supports precious metals and gemstone trading through trusted supplier relationships, UAE refinery partnerships, and secure logistics across local and international markets.

Precious Metals Trading

Trading bullion bars, doré, scrap gold, and semi-processed precious metals including gold, silver, platinum, and palladium through approved supplier networks and institutional market participants.

Bullion Supply

Procurement of high-purity gold bars (995 and 9999 fineness) from UAE suppliers.

Refinery Coordination

Processing locally sourced scrap gold through UAE refineries into standardized bullion.

Pearls & Precious Stones Trading

Supply of certified diamonds, natural pearls, and colored gemstones including ruby, sapphire, and emerald sourced through verified international partners.

Certified Gemstones

Trading gemstones supported by recognized authenticity certification frameworks.

Responsible Sourcing

Supply chain alignment with international responsible sourcing standards.

International Commodity Trading

Import, export, and re-export of precious commodities supported by Dubai’s advanced logistics infrastructure and global trade connectivity.

Regional Distribution

Institutional Trade Support
