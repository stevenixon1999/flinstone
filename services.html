The full practice of the precious trade.

Sourcing and movement of precious metals and stones from refineries and mines, moved across international markets in every form they travel

Precious Metals Trading

Gold, silver, platinum, and palladium, sourced directly from accredited refineries and mines. Traded in refined bullion, doré, and raw form, and placed with institutional buyers across global markets.

Bullion Supply

DGD-standard bars and 995 / 9999 fineness bullion across all four metals.

Raw & Doré

Scrap, doré, and semi-processed lots moved into the refining chain through established partners.

Precious Stones & Pearls

Certified diamonds, coloured stones, and pearls — sourced at origin, graded before they move, and delivered with full documentation from cutter to buyer.

Certified Stones

Diamonds and coloured gemstones with GIA, IGI, or HRD grading, verified before placement.

Uncut & Natural Material

Rough diamonds under Kimberley Process compliance, and pearls across Akoya, South Sea, Tahitian, and freshwater origins.

International Trade

Import, export, and re-export of precious metals and stones across global markets. Every leg structured with the right partners, permits, and counterparties.

Cross-Border Execution

End-to-end coordination of documentation, duty, and counterparty terms for each jurisdiction.

Market Access

Direct relationships with refineries, mines, and institutional buyers across the Middle East, Asia, and Europe.

Customs & Clearance

Clearance support for high-value consignments on both ends of the route. Declarations, permits, and inspections handled by licensed brokers familiar with precious commodities.

Documentation

Commercial invoicing, certificates of origin, and Kimberley Process certification prepared to jurisdiction standards.

Regulatory Coordination

Liaison with customs authorities and tax agencies to move material cleanly through every port.

Transportation & Logistics

Insured movement of precious metals and stones, desk to vault and port to port. Routes planned, carriers vetted, material tracked until it's signed for.

Insured Freight

Air and ground transit with specialist carriers holding full valuables cover.

Chain of Custody

Verified handover at every leg, with documented sign-off from origin to delivery.

Vaulting & Custody

Short and long-term storage in accredited facilities. Held in segregated custody, audited on record, retrievable on notice.

Segregated Storage

Allocated vaulting in accredited facilities, held in the client's name.

Audit & Retrieval

Full audit trail, with access and redelivery coordinated through the desk.
